Who We Are?

At "Yoe Yar," we are more than just an e-commerce platform; we are the custodians of Myanmar's cultural heritage. Our journey began with a profound passion for preserving and sharing the beauty of authentic Burmese Traditional Products with the world.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the rich tapestry of Burmese culture is celebrated, where the skill and artistry of local artisans are recognized, and where traditional treasures find their way into the hearts and homes of individuals around the globe. Our vision is rooted in celebrating the cultural diversity of Myanmar. We aim to showcase the myriad traditions and art forms that make this nation truly extraordinary.

We also aspire to facilitate a global appreciation for Burmese craftsmanship and heritage.

Our Mission

Our mission is twofold: to curate an exclusive collection of Burmese Traditional Products that encapsulate the essence of Myanmar, and to provide a seamless and enriching online shopping experience that connects people with the soul of this extraordinary nation. We are dedicated to preserving the time-honored traditions of Myanmar. Through our mission, we empower local artisans to continue creating and passing down their craft to future generations. We also believe that cultural exchange promotes harmony and unity.

Our mission is to create a platform where cultures meet, stories are shared, and understanding flourishes.
